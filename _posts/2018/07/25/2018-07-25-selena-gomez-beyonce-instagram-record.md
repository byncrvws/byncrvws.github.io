The reigning queen of Instagram, Selena Gomez has smashed a major social media milestone, beating Beyoncé and as a Selenator I am popping the fkn champagne (don’t even @ me, BeyHive).

<img src="https://media.giphy.com/media/NgGb0WbpfNUNW/giphy.gif" width="422" height="230" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;">

The now 26-year-old has held the impressive title of most-followed ‘grammer since 2016 (she currently has more than 139 million followers) but the feat of clocking in the most likes in the shortest time was previously held by Queen Bey as her iconic bb announcement post went absolutely bonkers.

The upload that scored Bey the top slot is either the baby bump pic or the introduction photo of Sir and Rumi Carter, Elle reveals, with the former scoring 6.4 million likes in the span of about several hours, while the latter hit 7.8 million double-taps in its first 12 hours online.
