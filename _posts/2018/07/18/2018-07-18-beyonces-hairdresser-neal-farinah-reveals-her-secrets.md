BEYONCÉ HAS BECOME iconic for a myriad of reasons: selling over 100 million records worldwide, winning 22 Grammy Awards and being the most nominated woman in Grammy history, to name just a few of her many achievements.

She’s also become an icon for her glorious mane of hair that at times appears to have a life of it’s own.

![When Michelle Admitted She Wants to Be Beyoncé For a Day]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/2018-07-18-beyonces-hairdresser-neal-farinah-reveals-her-secrets-2.gif)

Anyone who has seen Beyoncé perform live knows the extent to which her hair is used as a prop. Her hair is flipped, whipped, twerked, twirled, thrown and blown about. Just type in ‘Beyoncé hair’ into giphy and you’ll have a kaleidoscope of magical moments of her hair werking it.
