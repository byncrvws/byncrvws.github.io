Even Bey and Jay need a break sometimes.

After their show in Poland, where Beyoncé found herself stuck on stage, the Carters took a well-deserved mini-vacation with their oldest daughter, Blue Ivy.

Bey took to Instagram to flaunt her off-duty style in a flowy orange floral dress and Ritch Erani PVC heels. She posted a photo and a video of her and husband Jay-Z relaxing and smiling on stone steps, with her shoes and a beer thrown to the side.
